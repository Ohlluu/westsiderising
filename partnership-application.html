WESTSIDE RISING Partnership Agreement

This is a partnership agreement between individuals, organizations, and institutions to work with WESTSIDE RISING as a PARTNER to build a just, livable, and vibrant Greater West Side.

Together, we will work to provide information, resources, and opportunities for capacity building, leadership development, civic engage, and other activities that support the activation of residents and stakeholders in West Side Communities.

As partners, we emphasize trust, honesty, transparency, accountability, and responsibility among ourselves and in the work we do together. Let's Partner!
