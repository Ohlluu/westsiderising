Youth Employment Services

Youth Programs Employment

About This Organization

Youth Employment Services is dedicated to connecting young people ages 16-24 with meaningful job training, comprehensive career counseling, and sustainable employment opportunities that help them build valuable skills and achieve financial independence.

Founded with the mission to break down barriers to employment for underserved youth, this organization has been a cornerstone of community development for over 15 years. We believe that every young person deserves the opportunity to discover their potential and build a successful future.

Services Offered

  • Job Readiness Training

    Comprehensive workshops covering resume writing, interview skills, workplace etiquette, and professional communication.

  • Career Counseling

    One-on-one guidance to help youth identify career paths, set goals, and create actionable plans for success.

  • Paid Internships

    Real-world work experience with local businesses and community organizations, providing valuable hands-on learning.

  • Job Placement Assistance

    Direct connections to employment opportunities with partner employers committed to hiring and supporting youth.

  • Financial Literacy Workshops

    Education on budgeting, saving, credit management, and financial planning for long-term stability.

  • Mentorship Program

    Matching youth with professional mentors who provide guidance, support, and encouragement throughout their career journey.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Ages 16-24
  • Resident of Chicago's West Side communities
  • Commitment to completing program requirements
  • No prior work experience necessary

Success Stories

Over the past year, Youth Employment Services has helped more than 500 young people gain employment, with 85% maintaining their jobs for at least 6 months. Participants report increased confidence, improved financial stability, and a clearer vision for their future careers.
