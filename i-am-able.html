I AM ABLE

Faith-based trauma-informed care from birth to end-of-life

Back to Resources

I AM ABLE is a faith-based trauma-informed care agency providing services from birth to the end-of-life. We are dedicated to responding to, recovering from, reducing and removing trauma and violence in our community.

Clinical Department

Provides therapeutic services to clients referred from the Department of Children and Family Services (DCFS), schools, hospitals, religious organizations, and more. We accept self-referrals and serve children and adults of all ages.

TR4IM Program

Our Trauma Response and Intervention Movement (TR4IM) is our social cohesion, public health, new community policing model. We are Responding to, Recovering from, Reducing and Removing Trauma and Violence 8-Blocks at a Time.

WELLNESS H.E.A.L.S.

I AM ABLE's Mental Health Crisis Response System for the North Lawndale Community. Available to ANYONE, ANYWHERE, ANYTIME (24/7/365). Designed to reduce emergency room visits and psychiatric hospitalization, as well as prevent legal and criminal justice involvement.
