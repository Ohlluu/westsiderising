Catholic Charities

Emergency shelter, rental, utility, and crisis services

Back to Resources

If you find yourself in need of emergency shelter, rental, utility, and other crisis services, Catholic Charities is here for you. Financial assistance may be available for people at risk of eviction or utility shutoff due to a short-term, one-time crisis.

Additionally, we connect people to other organizations that can help with emergency assistance in Chicago, Suburban Cook County, and Lake County.
