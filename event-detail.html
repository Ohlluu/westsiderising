Community Leadership Training

Saturday, March 15, 2024
2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
Community Center, 123 West Side Ave, Chicago, IL
Event Flyer

About This Event

Join us for an intensive leadership development workshop designed to empower community members with the skills, knowledge, and confidence needed to become effective leaders in the Greater West Side.

This comprehensive training session will cover essential leadership topics including community organizing, effective communication strategies, conflict resolution, and civic engagement. Participants will learn from experienced community leaders and have the opportunity to practice their skills through interactive exercises and group discussions.

What You'll Learn

  • Fundamentals of community organizing and grassroots mobilization
  • Effective communication and public speaking techniques
  • Building coalitions and collaborative partnerships
  • Navigating the political process and engaging with elected officials
  • Conflict resolution and consensus building
  • Strategic planning and campaign development

Who Should Attend

This workshop is open to all community members who are passionate about creating positive change in the Greater West Side. Whether you're new to community organizing or looking to enhance your leadership skills, this training will provide valuable insights and practical tools.

What to Bring

  • Notebook and pen for taking notes
  • Ideas and questions about community issues
  • Open mind and willingness to learn

Light refreshments will be provided. Please arrive 15 minutes early for registration and networking.
