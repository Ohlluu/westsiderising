Better Together Holiday Soiree

Thursday, December 4, 2025
5:45 PM
3020 W. Carroll, Chicago, IL

About This Event

You are invited to celebrate the season with us at our "Better Together" Holiday Soiree! Join WESTSIDE RISING for an elegant evening of community, celebration, and recognition as we come together to honor the spirit of the Greater West Side.

This special event brings together community members, partners, and supporters for an unforgettable evening featuring exceptional entertainment, delicious food and drinks, exciting prizes, and our prestigious Leadership Awards presentation.

Event Highlights

  • DJ entertainment throughout the evening
  • Food and drinks provided
  • Raffles and exciting prizes
  • Leadership Awards presentation
  • The West Side Orchestra strolling performance
  • The Best Dressed contest

Dress Code

Dress to impress in your finest dressy attire! We encourage guests to wear festive colors: Red, Black, and Gold. Show off your style and join us in celebrating in elegance.

All Are Welcome

This event is open to everyone in our community. Whether you're a long-time supporter or new to WESTSIDE RISING, we invite you to join us for this special celebration. Come together with neighbors, partners, and friends as we celebrate our community's achievements and look forward to a bright future.

Registration is encouraged. For more information or questions, please contact us at (872) 833-6848 or (773) 417-6605, or email wr.info@westsiderising.org
