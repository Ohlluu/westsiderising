Get Involved

Join us in building a stronger, more vibrant Greater West Side

Your Community Needs You

Whether you have a few hours a week or want to make a long-term commitment, there are countless ways to contribute to the Greater West Side. Every action, no matter how small, creates ripples of positive change in our community. Join us in building a just, livable, and vibrant future for all.

Ways to Make an Impact

Volunteer Your Time

Join our team of dedicated volunteers who support community events, mentor youth, assist with administrative tasks, and help with various programs throughout the year.

  • Event support and coordination
  • Youth mentorship programs
  • Office and administrative help
  • Community outreach initiatives

We're always looking for professionals willing to share their knowledge and skills. Whether you're in tech, education, legal, healthcare, or any other field, your expertise can make a difference.

  • Career counseling and guidance
  • Pro bono professional services
  • Guest speaking opportunities
