Know Your Rights

Everyone has rights, no matter their immigration status. It's important to be informed and prepared if approached by police, ICE, or immigration officials.

Enlace is committed to keeping our community safe, informed, and unified. You will find resources to help you:

  • Understand your legal rights in different situations
  • Access tools and hotlines for immediate support
  • Learn how to safely share information and protect your family

Please explore the resources below and share them with loved ones. Together, through knowledge and solidarity, we can protect our community.

Legal & Immigration Support

If you are looking for legal or immigration related support, Enlace's Legal Clinic partner Beyond Legal Aid has attorneys providing free over-the-phone legal advice.

Legal Helpline (872) 267-2252

Healthcare Consultation

Enlace's Promotorxs de Salud are available for consultation and connection to local healthcare providers.
