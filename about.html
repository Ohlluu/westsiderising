About Westside Rising

Empowering our community through training, capacity-building, and collective action

OUR HISTORY

WESTSIDE RISING started as a pilot project in 2017 and was later formed as a coalition in 2018 by a group of organizations and individuals. This collective of individuals and organizations are committed to working together to build an own-grown base of trained and informed leaders to represent the best interest of the Westside of Chicago. In 2021, WESTSIDE RISING formed a nonprofit organization.

Our Guiding Principles

The principles that guide our work are trust, integrity, honesty, transparency, accountability, and solidarity for our community, coalescence members, and our collective efforts.

Our Service Area

We focus our efforts in the following community areas: Lawndale, Garfield Park, Austin, and Humboldt Park.

OUR WORK

We provide capacity building, leadership development, organizing, and civic engagement training to amplify the voices and enhance the capacity, skill set, and knowledgebase of residents and leaders to better understand, navigate, and participate in organizing campaigns, the political process, and other efforts that support systemic change within Westside communities.

Community Voices Campaign

Each year we conduct intensive Boots-on-the-ground campaigns to hear directly from the community.

Youth Ambassadors

We provide SEL (social emotion training), leadership development, work readiness, civic engagement trainings and other opportunities.

Issue Areas

We work on issues that focus on equitable investments for Westside communities, violence prevention, park utilization, affordable housing and homelessness, opioid efforts, and amplifying community voices and abilities.
